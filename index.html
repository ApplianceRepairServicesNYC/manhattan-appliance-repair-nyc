Vented, Ventless, and Heat Pump Dryers

The diverse architecture of Manhattan often dictates the type of dryer that can be installed. We service all variations, including:

  • Vented Dryers: These traditional models expel hot, moist air through an exhaust vent to the outside. Common issues include clogged vents, which can be a fire hazard, and problems with the blower or heating element. Our repairs focus on clearing blockages and ensuring proper airflow for safe and efficient operation.
  • Ventless Dryers: Ideal for apartments without external venting options, these dryers condense moisture from the clothes, collecting it in a reservoir or draining it away. Our technicians are trained to diagnose and fix the specific issues that can arise with these advanced units, ensuring their complex systems operate optimally.
